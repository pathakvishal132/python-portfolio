InterviewPrepHub - AI-Driven Interview Preparation

InterviewPrepHub is an AI-powered platform designed to revolutionize interview preparation by offering personalized, domain-specific, and company-specific mock interviews. It aims to bridge the gap between theoretical learning and real-world interview experiences by providing instant feedback and structured learning resources.

Key Features:

  • AI-Generated Questions: Dynamic question generation based on domain, subdomain, and difficulty level.
  • Company-Specific Questions: Access previously asked interview questions categorized by experience level and job profile.
  • Real-Time Feedback: Get instant AI-generated feedback on text and voice responses, along with actual answers.
  • Search & Filter: Quickly search for specific companies and start targeted preparation.
  • Community Reviews: Read and write reviews about interview experiences at different companies.
  • User Profile & Progress Tracking: Maintain a personalized profile to track achievements and performance over time.

Future Enhancements:

  • Gamification with leaderboards, badges, and rewards.
  • Expanded dataset for more refined and adaptive learning.
  • AI-driven insights for personalized recommendations and learning paths.
  • Integration with hiring managers, educators, and industry experts.

Tech Stack: Angular, Django, Python, OpenAI API, PostgreSQL
